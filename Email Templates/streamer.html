Alpha Zoo is an adorable typing game for parents to play with kindergartners.

Build a zoo together. Learn to read by typing words. Feed animals and make them poop. Ride a ferris wheel. Throw hot dogs off the top if you want. Visit the gift shop, eat at the cafe, explore a little world together.
